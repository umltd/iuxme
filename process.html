The Ordering process

If you feel that we could, in any way, be of service to you, we request you to contact us. You are welcome to explain your requirements on the form provided. Alternatively, you can just leave your contact information (email id / phone number, depending on your preference) along with instructions (best time to call, specific queries etc.) and we will get back to you.

Based on your individual requirements, we will offer you with bespoke alternatives.

To provide you with unique products of the highest quality, we manufacture them on machines custom designed and made in-house. If you want a specific design, we will be happy to assist you. If, on the other hand, you would like us to work together to come up with a special product, we would love to be a part of the process. The designs may incorporate names, personal messages, special motifs etc., anything that would make the product more special for you and your loved ones.

Once you have decided what you would like, we will ensure that your product reaches you within 3 weeks.*

*   from the date of payment
