About us

We are a design shop offering bespoke services to the discerning outlook; Individuals with tastes that differ from the norm.

A small team with design philosophies well versed in the sub-continent's rich cultural traditions and equally, with the cutting edge of modern technologies, we include in our vocabulary designs inspired by both heritage and modernity.

Fulfilling your desire for products tailored to your tastes is most important to us. Whether you want a product with your own design, or you want a custom design from us, we will support you all the way. The ordering process is detailed here

We strongly believe that our products are about you, not about us or our brand. Hence, we do not normally brand products with our logo.
